Process

How it works?

ABB CS is a platform that allows you to perform coffee selection through cloud interface, ensuring the least lead time you would need to have a coffee brewed.

Scan

Open up the camera app on your device to scan through the QR code. A notification window of our website will pop up after scanning.

Browse

Click on the notification window to open up our webpage of ABB Coffee Selection.

Select

Hover to the selection tab on the top right of our website to begin your selection of coffee.

Serve

Click on "Start Brew" to instruct our robot to proceed with your selection. Your coffee will be done in no time!
